Overview

Escape to Cambodia's first national park, where "Happiness Mountain" offers a refreshing retreat from the tropical heat with its cool mountain climate and stunning pine forests. Located in Kampung Spue, Kirirom National Park features breathtaking waterfalls, diverse wildlife, and panoramic mountain views that create the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable camping adventure.

This 5-day, 4-night tour includes guided trekking through scenic trails, mountain biking, waterfall swimming, and evening campfires under the stars. With all camping equipment, meals, and experienced guides provided, you'll experience the best of Cambodia's natural wilderness while enjoying modern facilities and comfortable accommodations in this pristine mountain paradise.

What's Included / Excluded

Included

  • All meals during the tour (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
  • Professional English-speaking tour guide
  • Camping equipment (tent, sleeping bag, etc.)
  • Entrance fee to Kirirom National Park
  • Transportation from Phnom Penh and return

Excluded

  • Travel insurance
  • Personal expenses (souvenirs, snacks)
  • Tips for guide and driver
  • Alcoholic beverages

Tour Plan

  • Day 1: Pick up from Phnom Penh → drive to Kirirom → camp setup → forest walk
  • Day 2: Guided hike to waterfalls → lunch at riverbank → evening campfire
  • Day 3: Mountain biking trails → picnic lunch → optional wildlife spotting
  • Day 4: Cultural visit to local village → team building games at campsite
  • Day 5: Morning yoga → pack and return to Phnom Penh
